BASIC RULES

You will have a random character to draw within 1 minute. You will just have the characters name

DRAWING TIME

After starting the timer, you need to start drawing the character as far as you remember them, there are a bunch of them!

TIME IS UP!

Once the time has stopped, you will get an alert, stopping you from drawing further. Once everything is done you can play again!
